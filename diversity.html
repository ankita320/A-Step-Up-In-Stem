A Step Up In Stem

Importance of Diversity

Diversity in STEM is extremely important because with diversity, we can better work as a team and create strong ideas. With diversity, we can create something better and even greater. We can better work as a team and trully create teamwork. We can share our ideas and encourage others. Through diversity, we can wipe off the STEM stereotypes and instead share more motivational ideas.

The statistics right now show that there are more men in the STEM field than women. Through diversity, we can change that. We can make it so that there are equal people in the STEM field without a majority of one gender or race. And with the diversity, we can create big things that would not be created without diversity.
