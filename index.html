A Step Up In Stem

Our mission is to inspire people from all over the world to be encouraged to join the STEM field. We want them to not be discouraged and join with confidence. We want to create diversity in the STEM field and do so that people are not afraid and are instead brave to join. We want to empower others and give the opportunties avalible to them. Creating diversity in the STEM field is our main goal because there tends to be stereotypes between gender roles and etc. We want to erase those stereotypes. We choose to empower, motivate, and inspire.

Empower. Motivate. Inspire.
